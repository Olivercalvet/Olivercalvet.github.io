About Us

RedBird is a new kind of rideshare app—built on fairness, freedom, and transparency. We started RedBird because we saw a broken system: drivers losing up to 40% of every fare, riders frustrated by rising prices and unclear fees, and no real say on either side.

We’re changing that.

RedBird gives power back to people. Drivers subscribe for a flat monthly fee and keep 100% of what they earn. No commissions. No cuts. Just a fair system that rewards effort.

Riders benefit too—every ride starts with a suggested fare, but both rider and driver can negotiate. That means pricing adapts naturally to local conditions like demand, distance, or difficulty, without needing hidden surge pricing or complex algorithms.

It’s price self-regulation, driven by the community.
No one gets squeezed. Everyone gets a voice.

With RedBird, you’re not just booking a ride—you’re choosing a better system. A transparent one. A human one. One where drivers are treated like partners, and riders get better service at fair prices.

We’re building a platform where trust, choice, and community come first. Fair rides. Better pay. And a new standard for how apps should treat people.
