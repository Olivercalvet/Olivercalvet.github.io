ABOUT US

PARAGUAYAN EMPANADAS

Welcome to Empanadas

We take pride in bringing a piece of Paraguay to your table. Founded in 2025, our little online store specializes in selling authentic Paraguayan empanadas, made with traditional recipes that have been passed down from generation to generation. Whether you are looking for a delicious appetizer for your next celebration, a special dish for a family gathering, or simply a personal treat, our empanadas are the perfect choice.

Our mission

Our mission is to make Paraguayan empanadas accessible to everyone, promoting the rich culinary heritage of Paraguay.

We want every customer to enjoy the warmth and flavor of our empanadas, whether at a party, a family gathering, or simply in the comfort of their home.

Who are we?

We are a team passionate about Paraguayan cuisine and sharing our culture with the world.

Every empanada we offer is handmade with fresh, high-quality ingredients, ensuring an authentic and delicious taste. We strive to provide exceptional service and a unique shopping experience because we believe that every bite should be memorable.

What do we offer?

We offer a variety of empanadas filled with the most traditional flavors and some modern twists. From our classic beef and chicken empanadas to vegetarian options, there is something for everyone.

Additionally, we offer personalized services for special events, ensuring that your celebration is unforgettable.
